The Flash

About The Flash

The Flash is a superhero who helps others in his city of residence, Central City. He helps the city cope through stuggles such as meta-humans like him. Meta-humans are those people who have recieved powers from the particle accelerator which blew some months back. The Flash tries to help those in trouble from bad meta-humans and keeps Central City hoping for a better day.

The Flash and his team!

The Flash's Characteristics

  1. He is the FASTEST man alive!
  2. He wears a red suit!
  3. There are always new additions to his team, Team Flash!
  4. He has defeated many villains throughout his time as the Flash!
  5. He is a CSI at the Central City Police Department (CCPD)!
  6. He is trying to prtect his city, Central City!

The Flash's Friends and Family

The Flash has made many different types of friends over the years. He has become friends with his superhero squad who work together to make the world a better place and solve crisis's. The Flash also has a team called "Team Flash" who work with him to prevent meta-humans from hurting others. His best friend would probably would be Cisco Ramon (vibe) who works with him at Star Labs along with Team Flash and he was a meta-human until he gave up his powers.
